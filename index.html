Frequently Asked Questions

What is a Car Check?

A car check is a report that provides detailed information about a vehicle’s history, including accident reports, title issues, previous owners, and service records. It helps you make an informed decision when buying or selling a car.

How do I perform a Car Check?

Simply Choose one of our plan. You’ll receive a detailed report within seconds.

Why should I check a car before buying a used car?

A car check provides you with important information about a vehicle’s history, such as whether it’s been in an accident, if there are any title issues, or if the odometer has been rolled back. This ensures you don’t buy a car with hidden problems.

How accurate is the car check report?

Our reports are sourced from trusted databases and cover a wide range of vehicle history information. We strive to provide the most accurate and up-to-date data available.
